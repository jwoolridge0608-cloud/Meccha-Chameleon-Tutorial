Let's Go Over The Basics!!

Welcome! This tutorial will teach you the basics of movement, hunting, camouflage, and each game mode before you play for the first time.

Start Learning

Movement

Move around the map to hide or while searching for opponents.

  • WASD to Move, hold SHIFT to Run
  • Mouse to Look Around
  • SPACE BAR to Jump
  • Hold SPACE BAR against walls to climb

How to Hunt

Observe your surroundings and identify suspicious objects.

  • Inspect carefully, check all nooks and perspectives
  • Listen for taunts, Hiders might whistle every once and awhile
  • Eliminate hidden players by shooting them with your paint gun
  • Work with teammates to clear sections of the maps

Camouflage

Blend into your environment using paint abilities.

  • Open Paint Mode by pressing the F key
  • Hold SPACE BAR to color pick and match surrounding colors
  • Hide in creative locations using R to pose
  • Stay still when enemies are nearby

Game Modes

Normal Mode

Classic hide and hunt gameplay.

Infection Mode

Players join the hunters after being found.

Double Mode

All players hide, then all players hunt. First to find everyone wins.

Increasing Tag

When caught, you become it. Hide until the end to win.

Reverse Chicken Race

One by one, each player's appearance is revealed before each hunt.

Tutorial Complete!

You are now ready to begin your first match. Good luck and remember to blend in!
